the capture card is now in my possesion, and it works perfectly fine. if i were to recommend a capture device for digitizing vhs tapes, just look up usb composite tv tuners. tv tuners wont care if the composite signal gets terrible unlike some other composite to usb capture devices, which go blank if theres a speck of dust in the video (i may be overexagerating it, but if the signal:noise ratio is moderately to the noise side the video goes blank. this especially happens on those cheap easycap generic composite to usb devices)
