the thought.

for the longest time i've always wanted a website of my own but never knew where to start (i did at some point make something in wix and carrd.co (now lost to time) but those never went anywhere). thats when one faithful day i was browsing protoweb's geocities archived sites, and thats where it hit me. "neocities..." i never even heard of neocities, and somehow those two words materialized in my head. that thought started all of this.

idiot's first lines of html


i think the fact that i have to write my own html to make site is what got me here in the first place. neocities was like a breath of fresh air! me, in control of everything in ways i never imagined before, all for the low low price of air. it was exactly what i'd been looking for all this time!
